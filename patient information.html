Patient Information

Patient Information

Helpful information to support your care at our practice

Registering with the Practice

New patients are welcome to register with our practice. Registration forms can be completed online or collected from reception. Proof of address and identification may be requested, but lack of documentation will not prevent registration.

Appointments

Appointments can be booked online, by phone, or in person at reception. We offer routine, urgent, and same-day appointments depending on availability and clinical need.

If you are unable to attend your appointment, please cancel as early as possible so it can be offered to another patient.

Prescriptions

Repeat prescriptions can be requested via our dispensary if you are a dispensing patient, or by submitting a request at reception. Please allow 72 hours for processing before collecting your medication.

Test Results

Test results are usually available within a few days to a week. Results can be viewed online or discussed with a clinician if follow-up is required. You will be contacted if action is needed

Your Medical Records

You have the right to access your medical records. Online services allow you to view parts of your record, test results, and appointment history. Please speak to reception for more information.

Confidentiality

We are committed to protecting your privacy. All personal and medical information is handled confidentially in accordance with data protection laws.

Feedback and Complaints

We welcome feedback to help improve our services. If you have a concern or complaint, please contact the practice and we will do our best to resolve it promptly and fairly.

Contact Us

If you need further information or assistance, please contact the practice by phone, email, or by visiting reception.
